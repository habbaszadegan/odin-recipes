Oatmeal

You want Oats?! I'll give you Oats!

Need an easy and fast breakfast solution? Oatmeal might just be the perfect answer for you!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Chia seeds
  • Alternative milk
  • Banana
  • Apple
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Combine oats, chia seeds and alt milk in a pot.
  2. Heat on stove for 4-5 minutes, stirring regularly.
  3. Once finished, add honey, banana and apple.
