Veggie Omelette



The Perfect Veggie Omelette

The perfect veggie omelette is minutes away from hitting your breakfast table. With a few humble ingredients from your local farmers market, be ready to prepare this delicious and nutritious meal in no time at all.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Zuchini
  • Tomato
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Chop veggies
  2. Add oil
  3. Sautee
  4. Add spices
  5. Add eggs
  6. Enjoy!
