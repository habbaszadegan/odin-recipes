Fruit Platter

Feeling like a fruit bat? I got just the thing for you!

Maybe you ate a cow the night before and are bloated. Maybe you just don't want your tummy to deal with too much. This fruit platter will be your best friend.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Oranges
  • ...really any fruit you want

Steps

  1. Chop em, dice em, put em in a cake. Serve them however you like!
  2. Just don't show a banana to an Arsalan in the wild. That critter will steal that banana in the blink of an eye.
